Affirmations

Store all your affirmations in one place with a simple, easy-to-use interface.

Affirmations can be a life-transforming technique for reprogramming your subconscious mind, with endless applications in all areas of your life.

An affirmation practice consists in writing down personal, carefully worded affirmations that powerfully resonate with you, and repeating them daily over long periods of time.

With Affirmations, you can store all your affirmations and quotes in one place with a simple and easy-to-use interface. Get it on Google Play!

Simple and beautiful material UI.

Keep your affirmations and quotes organized in folders with a simple, easy-to-use interface. Editing, sorting and moving affirmations around is also easy and intuitive.

Read your affirmations in fullscreen.

Get rid of all distractions and use the whole screen to read your affirmations with a large font. Use autoplay and player controls to customize the way you read your affirmations.

Dark theme/Night mode.

Use the app at night with the available dark theme. You can also enable true black backgrounds for devices with AMOLED screens.

Protect the app with a passcode.

Enable passcode or fingerprint lock to protect your affirmations and be the only one with access to the app.
